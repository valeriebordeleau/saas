TypeRights is a user friendly font management system. Increase productivity by organizing your fonts by usage rights, jobs, and many other criteria. TypeRights is ideal for a studio atmosphere, freelancer’s lifestyle, or the just starting-out student.

Usage Rights

Never again fumble, trying to figure out if you can legally use that beautiful typeface you just rediscovered. TypeRights will sort your fonts by usage rights to help you find the perfect typeface for the task at hand.

Search Fonts

Sorting through hundreds of font families can be a real challenge. TypeRights quickly searches through your font libraries to help you find the perfect typeface. TypeRights can search by classification, mood, usage rights, or foundry.

Sync Fonts

Working with a team? TypeRights can be synced to multiple devices and users. Insure consistency by making sure the whole team has the proper typeface where ever they may happen to be working.

Make Notes

Stay connected with the team. TypeRights allows users to leave notes for other uses. Quickly alert the whole team to any changes on a project or leave a reminder for yourself to insure you apply the typeface the client has selected.
